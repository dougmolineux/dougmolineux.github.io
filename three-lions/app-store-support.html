How the tournament works

You guide England through a three-match cup run. Beat Ghana in Round 1, Germany in the semifinal, and Argentina in the final to win the World Cup.

Round 1: Ghana Semifinal: Germany Final: Argentina

How instructions work

During the match you can shout tactical prompts that influence England's movement, spacing, pressure, and shooting behavior.

spread out everyone defend attack quickly let #9 shoot

AI modes

The game includes Original Mode and a short-horizon on-device Foundation batch mode. If the Foundation option is not supported on a device, Three Lions automatically falls back to Original Mode.

  • Original Mode uses built-in tactical logic and prompt parsing.
  • Foundation batch mode plans short movement sequences on supported devices.
  • Gameplay continues even if the advanced mode is unavailable.

Progress and celebration

Your manager name and championship goal-difference results are stored on-device. Winning the final unlocks a full celebration screen and adds your run to the persistent leaderboard.

  • Leaderboard entries track goal difference across the full cup run.
  • Repeat wins build a longer history for the same manager name.
  • You can restart the whole tournament from Settings.

Troubleshooting

  • If the match appears frozen, close the instruction sheet or settings sheet. Gameplay pauses while those panels are open.
  • If advanced AI mode is missing, your device likely does not support the on-device Foundation feature used by the app.
  • If you want a tactical reset, use halftime to submit a fresh instruction before the second half begins.
  • If you want to start over from the beginning, use Restart Entire Game in Settings.

Compatibility notes

  • Three Lions is an iOS app built for quick portrait play sessions.
  • The project is currently configured for iOS 26.0 and later.
  • The advanced Foundation batch mode only appears on supported devices.
  • Match logic and AI behaviors are designed to run on-device.

Frequently asked questions

How do I win the World Cup in Three Lions?

Win three consecutive knockout matches as England: Ghana first, Germany second, and Argentina in the final. If England loses, the tournament resets back to Ghana.

What kind of instructions can I give the team?

England responds to short tactical prompts such as spreading out, defending deep, pressing, attacking, or favoring a specific shirt number like #9.

Why does the game pause when I open a menu?

The live match pauses whenever the instruction editor or settings screen is open so you can make changes without losing game time.

Why do I not see the Foundation AI option?

That mode only appears on devices that support the on-device Foundation capabilities required by the app. Unsupported devices continue using Original Mode automatically.
