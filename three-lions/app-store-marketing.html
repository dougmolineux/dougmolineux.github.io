Why Three Lions feels different

Live tactical prompts

Tell England to spread out, sit deeper, press, attack, or route the ball toward a specific shirt number. The team changes shape and behavior based on what you say.

Halftime matters

The match resets for the second half and gives you another moment to adjust your approach before the game restarts.

Distinct opponents

Ghana, Germany, and Argentina give the tournament a clear ladder, escalating pressure, and a satisfying final-step payoff.

Win and leave a mark

Lifting the cup triggers a full celebration sequence and records your run in a persistent goal-difference leaderboard.

Three matches. One clean route to glory.

Every run is simple to understand and hard to finish. Lose at any stage and you are back at the start. Win all three and the celebration is yours.

Round 1

England vs Ghana

England vs Germany

Survive the last match and Three Lions erupts into a trophy lift, confetti, and a permanent leaderboard entry.

On-device AI, arcade energy

Three Lions includes an original tactical simulation mode and a short-horizon on-device planning mode on supported hardware. Either way, the result is the same: quick decisions, visible reactions, and a match that feels like it is listening when you intervene.
