NotebookLM AI Research Tool

3/7/2025

What is NotebookLM?

NotebookLM is a free tool created by Google described as:

NotebookLM is a research and note-taking tool that uses AI to help you interact with your documents. It's designed to help you read, take notes, and organize your ideas.

I think you may need a google account, but otherwise I have found all the features to be free.

How does it work?

You can upload various files and links, even local large documents. You can provide it URL links to articles and YouTube video links. It will digest this information and provide you with an AI chat bot that you can use to ask it to summarize, or other random questions about the content. I have found the AI isn't incredibly impressive compared to other LLMs I have used.

Audio Commentary

This is where this tool shines. It will create audio commentary (basically a podcast) about the content that you have provided it.
