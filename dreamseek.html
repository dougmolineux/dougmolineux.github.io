DreamSeek

Your private dream journal & analyzer

About The App

DreamSeek helps you record and analyze your dreams while maintaining complete privacy. Unlike other apps, all processing happens directly on your device - your dreams never leave your iPhone.

100% On-Device Processing

Key Features

  • Voice Recording - Speak your dreams upon waking
  • Theme Detection - Automatically identifies adventure, romance, nightmares and more
  • Sentiment Analysis - Tracks emotional tone of your dreams
  • People & Places - Shows who and where appears most frequently
  • Visual Analytics - Interactive charts reveal patterns over time
  • No Cloud Processing - All analysis happens locally

How To Use

  1. Tap the microphone button when you wake up
  2. Describe your dream out loud
  3. Review the automatic analysis
  4. Add optional title or notes
  5. Track patterns over time in your dashboard

Privacy Policy

DreamSeek does not collect, store, or transmit any of your dream data. All processing occurs locally on your device.

We do not:

  • Upload your dreams to any servers
  • Use third-party analytics
  • Sell or share your data
